KeyPose v0.5

Browser-based keyframe animation for GLTF/GLB characters. Pose bones, animate keyframes, export storyboards.

🎬 Launch Web App ⬇️ Download Desktop

KeyPose timeline and bone controls

🦴 Bone Posing

Click bone markers, use Move/Rotate controls. Full transform control for every bone in your GLTF/GLB model.

🎞️ Keyframe Timeline

Set keyframes, drag hold arrows, choose linear/step interpolation. Camera follows "ones", character respects holds.

📸 Storyboard Panels

Capture poses as panels, reorder, rename. Export as PDF with manga margins or full frame sequence with production labels (A, A1, B...).

🎨 Shader Effects

Cel shading, exposure, greyscale, gradient maps. Real-time, preserved in exports.
